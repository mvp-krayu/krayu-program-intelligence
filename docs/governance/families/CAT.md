**Status:** REGISTERED
**Registered:** 2026-04-04

## PURPOSE

Category authority, construct positioning, derivative classification, and reinforcement of canonical conceptual surfaces. CAT streams establish how constructs are positioned relative to each other and relative to the canonical layer.

---

## STANDARD INVARIANTS

- Category authority originates from the canonical layer — no bottom-up authority claims
- Derivative constructs must be explicitly positioned relative to canonical
- No promotional drift in authority statements
- Positioning must be traceable to a canonical source
