| # | Deliverable | Description | Format |

| 1 | **LENS Access** | Interactive structural intelligence surface. BOARDROOM and BALANCED cognitive modes. Structural topology, pressure zones, named findings, Software Intelligence consequence analysis. | Web application — secure access credentials provided |
| 2 | **Structural Assessment Package** | Governed export containing: Structural Verdict (9-chapter executive document), Structural Topology (embedded visual), Evidence Record (posture, confidence, governance boundary). | Self-contained HTML document |
| 3 | **Advisory Delivery Session** | Walkthrough of findings with Signäl team. Topology exploration, finding review, consequence analysis, verdict presentation, Q&A. | Video conference or in-person. 60-90 minutes. |

### Deliverable Governance

All deliverables are produced by the Signäl structural intelligence runtime through governed export actions. Findings are deterministically derived from structural evidence. The same evidence produces the same findings.

Signäl does not manually compose, edit, suppress, or augment runtime-generated findings. Advisory interpretation during the delivery session provides context and operational framing — the underlying intelligence is runtime-produced.

|-------|--------------|------------|
| **Evidence Intake** | Day 1 | Customer provides repository access per Evidence Intake Checklist. Signäl verifies access and confirms evidence sufficiency. |

## 7. Confidentiality

### Signäl Obligations

- Customer source code is processed for structural topology extraction only
- Source code is not retained after the engagement unless explicitly agreed in writing
- Structural evidence artifacts (topology, metrics, qualification state) are retained for deliverable production and are subject to the confidentiality terms of this agreement
- Signäl will not disclose customer identity, program details, or assessment findings to third parties without written consent

### Customer Obligations

- Signäl deliverables (Structural Assessment Package, LENS access) are provided for Customer's internal use
- Customer may share the Structural Assessment Package with their board, steering committee, investment committee, or internal stakeholders
- Customer may not redistribute Signäl deliverables to third parties, publish them externally, or use them for competitive benchmarking against Signäl without written consent

### Mutual

- Both parties agree to protect the other's confidential information with at least the same care used for their own confidential information
- This obligation survives termination of the engagement for a period of [CONFIDENTIALITY PERIOD — e.g., 2 years]

## 8. Methodology Disclosure

Signäl's structural intelligence is derived through a governed cognition runtime that:

- Extracts structural topology from code evidence (files, dependencies, module boundaries)
- Derives structural conditions through deterministic analysis (same evidence → same findings)
- Qualifies conclusions through governed semantic review with explicit acceptance and rejection
- Produces intelligence artifacts with full evidence lineage and governance boundary disclosure
- Uses bounded AI participation (~14% of output) exclusively for audience-appropriate narrative projection — disclosed and governance-bounded

The runtime does not: execute customer code, access runtime environments, analyze application data, infer organizational behavior, attribute findings to individuals, or prescribe specific remediation actions.
