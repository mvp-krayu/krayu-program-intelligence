## 2. Value-Based Pricing Model

Value-based pricing anchors to the economic value delivered, not to cost or market averages.

### Primary Value Scenarios

**Scenario A — Pre-transformation commitment (most common trigger)**
- Program value at risk: €5M–€50M
- Cost of committing to wrong path: 15–40% of program cost wasted = €750k–€20M
- LENS assessment cost as % of risk avoided: 0.03%–0.5%
- Implied willingness to pay: €30k–€150k for T1

**Scenario B — Pre-investment / M&A technical due diligence**
- Deal size: €10M–€200M
- Cost of technical risk undiscovered: 5–20% deal erosion = €500k–€40M
- Traditional due diligence cost: €75k–€200k
- LENS at €17k–€35k is dramatically underpriced relative to reference alternative

**Scenario C — Recurring governance (T3 trigger)**
- Annual program cost under governance: €5M–€30M
- Value of structural visibility: 3–8% cost avoidance = €150k–€2.4M/yr
- Implied T3 retainer value: €150k–€500k/yr

### Economic Value Capture Model

| Tier | EVC Low | EVC High | Current Price | Capture Rate | Recommended Price |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| T1 | €50k | €500k | €17k–€35k | 3–7% | €25k–€55k |
| T2 | €100k | €800k | €35k–€95k | 4–12% | €55k–€130k |
| T3 | €300k+ | €3M+/yr | Not defined | — | €180k–€450k/yr |

At current pricing, Signäl captures 3–7% of delivered EVC. Mature B2B enterprise products typically capture 15–30%. There is significant room to move upward without hitting buyer resistance.

## 3. Cost-Plus Analysis (Floor Pricing)

This establishes minimum sustainable pricing, not recommended pricing.

### T1 Cost Structure (per engagement)

| Cost element | Low | High |
|---|---|---|
| Analyst / derivation time (60–100 hrs @ €120–180/hr) | €7,200 | €18,000 |
| Technology infrastructure | €400 | €800 |
| Pre-sales / scoping (10–15 hrs) | €1,500 | €2,700 |
| Delivery overhead (QA, governance, review session) | €2,000 | €4,000 |
| **Total COGS** | **€11,100** | **€25,500** |

### Margin Analysis at Current Pricing

| Price | COGS (mid) | Gross Margin |
|---|---|---|
| €17,000 | €18,000 | -6% (below floor on complex engagements) |
| €25,000 | €18,000 | 28% |
| €35,000 | €18,000 | 49% |
| €50,000 | €18,000 | 64% |

**Key finding:** T1 floor of €17k is below break-even on complex environments. The floor should be €22k minimum. Any engagement quoted below €25k requires explicit scope containment.

T2 is investigative — scope variance is high. Floor: €40k. Target: €65k–€95k for Phase 1. Avoid fixed pricing; scope-linked pricing is appropriate.

## 4. Price Elasticity Estimate

### Demand Characteristics

Signäl LENS is a highly inelastic product in its target segment:

- Buyers are C-suite or board-level — price is not the primary decision variable
- The decision is trust-based, not budget-based, until ~€200k+
- The purchase is triggered by a specific event (transformation, investment, incident) — it is not discretionary
- There is no volume purchasing — one engagement at a time

### Estimated Price Elasticity by Range

| Price Range | Elasticity | Interpretation |
|---|---|---|
| €15k–€40k | -0.2 to -0.4 | Very low sensitivity — price barely affects demand |
| €40k–€100k | -0.4 to -0.6 | Moderate sensitivity — enters procurement scrutiny |
| €100k–€200k | -0.6 to -0.9 | Approaching budget approval threshold |
| €200k+ | -1.0 to -1.5 | Full procurement process, board approval likely needed |

At current pricing, price is not the barrier — positioning and trust are. Moving T1 from €17k to €35k will not measurably reduce close rate.

## 5. Psychological Pricing Strategies

### Anchoring

The most powerful lever available. Currently underused.

- Open every commercial conversation with T3 programme value (€150k–€500k/yr)
- Present T1 as "the entry point to understanding whether T2 or T3 is warranted"
- Legitimate anchor: "most clients commit €200k+ in transformation cost before engaging us"
- Do not lead with T1 pricing — it anchors at the wrong level
- Do not present the tiering ladder bottom-up

### Decoy Pricing

T2 at €35k–€95k is too broad (2.7× spread) — signals pricing uncertainty rather than structured packaging. Tighten:

- T2 Phase 1: €55k–€75k (bounded, named)
- T2 Phase 2: €85k–€130k (add TRACE, consequence class)

This makes T2 Phase 1 look accessible relative to Phase 2, creating a natural internal decoy.

### Charm Pricing

For enterprise B2B, round-number charm pricing is irrelevant. However:

- Use price fences, not round numbers: "€47,500" feels more calculated and justified than "€50,000"
- Avoid price ranges in formal proposals — a range signals uncertainty; quote a specific number with scope justification

### The Subtraction Frame

Instead of "T1 costs €35k," frame as: "A T1 assessment represents approximately 0.3% of the program cost at risk — with full structural grounding before commitment." This reframes cost as a percentage of risk, not an absolute number.

## 6. Tiering Recommendation

### Recommended Tier Architecture

**Tier 1 — LENS Assessment** — *Decision-ready structural view*

- Price: €25,000–€50,000 (scope-linked, quoted per engagement)
- Included: Evidence intake, structural derivation, executive report, guided review session
- Decision output: PROCEED / INVESTIGATE / ESCALATE
- Delivery: 3–4 weeks
- Positioning: Entry point. Standalone value. Not a loss leader.

**Tier 2 — Diagnostic Access (Phase 1)** — *Evidence gap layer, bounded investigation*

- Price: €55,000–€85,000 (scope-linked)
- Included: Diagnostic zone inventory, WHY + EVIDENCE modes, Diagnostic Narrative HTML export, topology graph
- Triggered by: INVESTIGATE decision state from T1
- Delivery: 4–6 weeks
- Positioning: The answer to "what do I investigate next"

**Tier 3 — Enterprise Access** — *Continuous structural intelligence*

- Price: €150,000–€400,000/yr (programme-based retainer)
- Included: L2–L4 derivation access, governed contract, continuous interrogation capability, quarterly structural reviews
- Triggered by: ESCALATE, or T1/T2 establishing ongoing structural risk
- Positioning: A governance-layer function, not a project

### What's Currently Missing

- **T1.5 / Assessment Plus**: T1 output with a brief validation round (evidence QA, second review session). Price: €40k–€60k. Captures the gap between T1 and T2 without full diagnostic access.
- **Advisory retainer add-on at T1**: €8k–€15k/yr for monitoring the decision after assessment. Low effort, recurring revenue.

## 7. Discount Strategy

### When to Discount

| Condition | Discount | Mechanism |
|---|---|---|
| First engagement with new client | 10–15% | Relationship pricing, not advertised |
| Reference client (case study rights) | 15–20% | Documented, exchanged for specific deliverable |
| Multi-engagement commitment (T1+T2 upfront) | 10% | Bundled pricing |
| PE/VC firm (multiple portfolio companies) | 20–25% | Master services agreement, volume commitment |
| Academic / research partner | 30–40% | Non-commercial rate, explicit classification |

### When Never to Discount

- For urgency ("if we sign by Friday") — destroys perceived value
- For unqualified leads who push back on price early (signals misfit, not opportunity)
- Below cost floor (sub-€22k for T1)
- Without a specific exchange (reference, commitment, volume)

### Discount Authority Levels

| Discount | Authority |
|---|---|
| 0–10% | Account manager discretion |
| 10–20% | Requires commercial sign-off |
| 20%+ | Requires written justification and strategic rationale |

## 9. Monetization Opportunities

### Upsells

| Upsell | Trigger | Price |
|---|---|---|
| Assessment Plus (second review session + evidence QA) | Client wants more interpretation after T1 | €8k–€15k add-on |
| T2 Phase 2 (TRACE mode, consequence class) | T2 Phase 1 surfaces need for deeper traversal | €30k–€55k uplift |
| Re-assessment (12 months post T1) | Program evolved; prior assessment no longer current | 60–70% of original T1 price |
| Additional domain spotlight | T1 identifies secondary high-risk domain | €12k–€20k per domain |

### Cross-sells

| Cross-sell | Mechanism | Price |
|---|---|---|
| Structural monitoring retainer | T1/T2 client wants ongoing signal monitoring | €15k–€25k/yr |
| Board briefing preparation | Translate assessment output for board pack | €8k–€12k |
| Vendor/partner structural review | Same lens applied to key suppliers | T1-equivalent |
| Programme onboarding baseline | New program director wants structural baseline at start | T1-equivalent |

### Usage-Based / Variable Pricing (T3)

| Model | Description | Price signal |
|---|---|---|
| Per-domain interrogation | Charged per domain scope expansion | €8k–€15k per domain added |
| Query volume tier | Workspace access priced by query depth | Bundle tiers within retainer |
| Evidence refresh fee | Re-ingestion when evidence base changes significantly | €10k–€20k per refresh |

### Strategic Channel: PE / VC Firm Relationships

A single relationship with a PE firm doing 5–8 portfolio acquisitions/year is worth €200k–€500k/yr at scale. Every acquisition triggers T1 (technical due diligence), high conversion to T2, and potentially T3 for portfolio-wide structural tracking.

**This is the highest-leverage channel to build first.**
