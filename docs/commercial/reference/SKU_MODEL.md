## Taxonomy

Five terms permanently distinguished:

| Term | Definition | Example |
|------|-----------|---------|
| **Product** | The commercial identity. What the market knows. One product exists. | Signäl |
| **SKU** | A purchasable unit. What appears on an invoice. Defines what the customer buys, what intelligence activates, and what surfaces they receive. | Signäl Structural Assessment |
| **Tier** | A commercial grouping of SKUs by depth of engagement. Tiers are not products — they are packaging envelopes. | Tier 1 (Assessment), Tier 2 (Continuous), Tier 3 (Enterprise) |
| **Surface** | A delivery mechanism through which intelligence reaches the customer. Surfaces are not products — they are consumption channels. | LENS, Structural Verdict, Evidence Record |
| **Deliverable** | A specific artifact the customer receives. Deliverables belong to surfaces. | 9-chapter Structural Verdict document, Evidence Record HTML, LENS access credentials |

**The rule:** A customer buys a SKU. The SKU activates intelligence. The intelligence is consumed through surfaces. Surfaces produce deliverables.

```
Customer → buys → SKU
                    ├── activates → Intelligence
                    └── consumed through → Surface(s)
                                            └── produces → Deliverable(s)
```

## Three-Layer SKU Architecture

### Layer 1: Engagement SKU (How the customer buys)

| Engagement Model | Description | Duration | Relationship |
|-----------------|-------------|----------|-------------|
| **ASSESSMENT** | Point-in-time structural intelligence engagement. One program. One analysis. Delivered. | Days (typically 3-5) | Per-engagement |
| **CONTINUOUS** | Ongoing structural intelligence access. Multiple runs. Self-service investigation. | Annual subscription | Ongoing |
| **ENTERPRISE** | Platform-level structural intelligence across programs. Operator authority. Custom modules. | Multi-year license | Strategic partnership |

### Layer 2: Intelligence SKU (What intelligence is activated)

Each intelligence layer is independently activatable. Layers are additive.

| Intelligence Layer | What It Reveals | Activation |
|-------------------|----------------|------------|
| **STRUCTURAL CORE** | Structural execution topology, domain mapping, cluster analysis, dependency edges, propagation paths, qualification posture. | Always included |
| **SOFTWARE INTELLIGENCE** | Structural conditions (11 types) + behavioral consequence analysis (9-slice ontology). Named findings: Execution Fragility, Coupling Inertia, Dependency Amplification, etc. Severity-classified. Topology-located. Consequence-attributed. | Always included (this IS the product) |
| **OPERATIONAL AUTHORITY** | Full operator evidence workbench, qualification workflow, governed escalation, 4-decimal precision, hash chain audit, RBAC-governed mutations. | Tier 3 only |

### Layer 3: Surface SKU (How intelligence is consumed)

| Surface | What It Is | Format |
|---------|-----------|--------|
| **LENS** | Interactive structural intelligence surface. 4 cognitive modes calibrated for different audiences. Topology exploration. Guided investigation. | Web application |
| **Structural Verdict** | Board-grade governed document. 9 chapters of named structural findings with evidence grounding. The artifact a CTO hands to their board. | Document (HTML/PDF) |
| **Evidence Record** | Governed structural evidence snapshot. Topology capture, posture assessment, confidence envelope, governance boundary. When guided queries have been explored, includes investigation trail: questions, evidence citations, governance boundaries. The auditable proof artifact. | Document (HTML) |

### Cognitive Modes (within LENS)

4 cognitive modes — same structural truth, different depths:

| Mode | Audience | What They See |
|------|----------|--------------|
| **BOARDROOM** | Board, C-suite, steering committee | Governed consequence projection — what the structure means for leadership |
| **BALANCED** | CTO, VP Engineering, Enterprise Architects | Operational causality — why the structure produces this outcome |
| **DENSE** | Technical leadership, principal engineers | Full structural mechanics — every signal, every derivation path. 76+ guided structural queries with evidence-traced answers. |
| **OPERATOR** | Engineers, analysts, auditors | Evidence inspection at full precision. Governance lifecycle audit. Signal verification at 4-decimal precision. |
