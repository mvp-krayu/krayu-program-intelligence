## Investment Committee Framing

### How to Present the Structural Verdict to IC

The Structural Verdict is designed for investment committee consumption. It is not a technical report reformatted for executives — it is executive intelligence derived from structural evidence.

**Recommended IC presentation structure:**

1. **Open with the posture.** PROCEED / INVESTIGATE / ESCALATE. This is the headline. The IC needs the governed conclusion first.

2. **Show the topology.** The target's structural execution architecture — domains, clusters, dependency edges, pressure zones. This is what the target's program actually looks like structurally. Most IC members have never seen this view.

3. **Walk through 3-5 highest-impact findings.** Each finding has four fields. For IC, emphasize: what it means for execution (field 3) and what it means for leadership/investment (field 4).

4. **Present the operational ceiling.** Is there a structural ceiling on execution velocity? Can it be resolved through isolated intervention, or does it require structural investment? This directly informs post-acquisition budget planning.

5. **Close with governance boundary.** What is confirmed, what is unverified, what is prohibited. The IC should understand exactly what the intelligence covers and what it does not claim.

### What the Structural Verdict Does NOT Contain

- No team assessments, organizational opinions, or personnel evaluation
- No remediation prescriptions or "you should" language
- No competitive benchmarking against industry averages
- No cost estimates for remediation
- No timeline projections for fixes

These are governed prohibitions, not omissions. The Structural Verdict reveals structural execution reality. It does not prescribe what to do about it — that is the deal team's judgment, informed by structural evidence.

## Investigation Trail — Appendix Format

The Investigation Trail is designed to be appended to investment committee deal packages as a structural evidence appendix.

### Contents

| Section | What It Contains |
|---------|-----------------|
| **Investigation Summary** | Number of queries executed, time span, governance boundary |
| **Query Log** | Each structural question asked, with: question text, evidence-traced answer, structural findings referenced, governance classification (confirmed / unverified / prohibited) |
| **Evidence Citations** | Every piece of structural evidence referenced during investigation, with: source location in topology, derivation path, confidence classification |
| **Governance Boundary** | What the investigation covered, what it did not cover, and what it is prohibited from claiming |

### Format Specifications

| Property | Value |
|----------|-------|
| **Format** | Self-contained HTML |
| **Dependencies** | None — renders in any browser without internet connection |
| **File naming** | `investigation-trail-[target]-[date].html` |
| **Archivability** | Yes — suitable for deal room, data room, compliance archive |
| **Appendability** | Designed as an appendix to IC packages. Can be referenced from the Structural Verdict. |
| **Confidentiality** | Subject to engagement agreement confidentiality terms |

### How It Integrates with IC Packages

```
INVESTMENT COMMITTEE PACKAGE
│
├── Deal Memo (deal team)
├── Financial Model (deal team)
├── Market Analysis (deal team)
```
