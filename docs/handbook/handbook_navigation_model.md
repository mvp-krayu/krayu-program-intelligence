PROGRAM INTELLIGENCE HANDBOOK
Handbook Navigation Model

Purpose

This artifact defines how the Program Intelligence Handbook should be navigated and how it connects the intellectual outputs of the Program Intelligence project streams.

The navigation model ensures that the handbook functions as a synthesis layer rather than a replacement for the authoritative artifacts produced in the streams.


NAVIGATION PRINCIPLE

The handbook is designed to guide the reader through the Program Intelligence discipline in a structured manner.

Each handbook section introduces a conceptual domain of the discipline and then directs the reader to the authoritative artifacts produced by the corresponding stream.

The handbook therefore operates as:

• a structured map of the discipline
• a synthesis narrative connecting the streams
• a navigation layer for the Program Intelligence body of knowledge


STREAM-TO-HANDBOOK RELATIONSHIP

Each stream contributes a domain of knowledge.

The handbook introduces that domain and explains its role in the overall discipline.

The detailed artifacts remain in the original stream directories.

The handbook references those artifacts rather than duplicating them.

CROSS-STREAM SYNTHESIS

Although the handbook introduces each stream sequentially, the discipline itself is interconnected.

Execution evidence flows through the discipline in the following conceptual chain:

Execution Evidence
→ Execution Signals
→ Signal Interpretation
→ Program Intelligence
→ Executive Insight


The handbook highlights these relationships and explains how the streams interact as a coherent system.


NAVIGATION MODES

Readers may use the handbook in two primary ways.


Sequential Reading

Readers follow the conceptual evolution of the discipline from doctrinal foundations toward real-world applications.

This path follows the natural structure of the handbook.


Domain Navigation

Readers may jump directly to the section corresponding to a particular domain of interest, such as signals, demonstrations, or case studies.

The handbook therefore functions as both a narrative publication and a reference map of the discipline.


HANDBOOK AS A DISCIPLINE MAP

The Program Intelligence Handbook ultimately serves as the map of the discipline.

It allows readers to understand:

• how the discipline is structured
• where specific knowledge domains reside
• how streams relate to one another
• how Program Intelligence forms a coherent body of knowledge


This navigation model ensures that the discipline remains organized while preserving the authority of the stream artifacts.
