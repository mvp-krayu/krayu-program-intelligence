The most significant cross-persona finding. The same 22 cognitive functions operate under FOUR fundamentally different orchestration models:

| Persona | Model | Mechanism | Driver | Cognitive Effect |

## COGNITIVE FUNCTION TRANSFORMATION LAWS

From the complete projection map, 7 laws governing how cognitive functions transform across personas:

### Law 1: The Compilation/Emergence Duality

Cognitive functions exist in one of two modes: EMERGENT (discovered at runtime from structural state) or COMPILED (deterministically produced from fixed logic). BALANCED is emergent. BOARDROOM is compiled. DENSE substitutes a THIRD mode: NAVIGATED (available everywhere, activated by operator position). INVESTIGATION introduces a FOURTH: SEQUENTIAL (forced order, no operator choice).

No persona combines emergence and compilation. These modes are mutually exclusive at the persona level.

### Law 2: The Decomposition Gradient

As cognitive altitude descends from BOARDROOM to INVESTIGATION, compound functions DECOMPOSE:

```
BOARDROOM:     #9 = single verdict ("2 structural tensions")
BALANCED:      #9 = one of 22 emergence functions (~15% of surface)
EXEC DENSE:    #9 = 4 zones: PZ + SA + AL + PF (~55% of surface)
INVESTIGATION: #9 = individual signal rows with per-signal evidence
```

Functions do not simply "expand" at lower altitudes. They DECOMPOSE into constituent parts that can be independently verified. Decomposition is not depth — it is structural disassembly.

### Law 3: The Interpretation/Prohibition Inversion

Functions that INTERPRET in BALANCED/BOARDROOM/DENSE become PROHIBITIONS in INVESTIGATION. Evidence Boundary (#14) is the clearest case: "confirmed unknowns" (interpretation of what evidence means) → "MUST NOT infer beyond evidence" (prohibition on what may NOT be concluded). Authority Mode (#22) follows the same pattern: disclosure/declaration/boundary (what we DO) → prohibition enumeration (what we CANNOT do).

### Law 4: The Synthesis Disappearance Rule

Synthesis functions (#4-7) are the FIRST to disappear in non-interpretive personas. INVESTIGATION loses Executive Synthesis (#4) and Compound Activation (#7) entirely. This is not a regression — synthesis produces MEANING from multiple inputs, and INVESTIGATION is constitutionally oriented toward VERIFICATION, not meaning production. A surface that synthesizes cannot simultaneously prove it didn't synthesize.

### Law 5: The Governance Depth Expansion

Governance-related functions (#12, #19, #22) EXPAND monotonically from BOARDROOM to INVESTIGATION:

```
#12 Governance Friction:
  BOARDROOM: legitimacy sentences → BALANCED: emergence event → DENSE: counts/rates → INVESTIGATION: full forensic tables

#19 Confidence Classification:
  BOARDROOM: passive consumption → BALANCED: tier logic → DENSE: raw Q-class → INVESTIGATION: 4-decimal detail

#22 Authority Mode:
  BOARDROOM: declaration → BALANCED: disclosure → DENSE: per-query → INVESTIGATION: prohibition + closure
```

Governance depth ONLY expands. No governance function is more detailed in BOARDROOM than in INVESTIGATION. This is not a design pattern — it reflects the constitutional reality that governance provenance requires MORE data at lower altitudes where claims must be individually verified.

### Law 6: The Module Embodiment Persistence

Domain Module embodiment is NEVER absent. Even in INVESTIGATION, which eliminates interpretation and synthesis, module terms persist as evidence subjects (domain_alias, signal domain, propagation role domain). The module is constitutionally irremovable because removing it produces structurally correct but operationally meaningless output.

### Law 7: The Absent Function Signature

Each persona's absent functions define its cognitive identity more precisely than its present functions:

| Persona | Absent Functions | Identity Revealed |
|---------|-----------------|-------------------|
| BOARDROOM | #1, #3, #6, #11, #16, #18, #20 | Eliminates emergence, meta-cognition, temporal, trail → VERDICT DELIVERY |
| BALANCED | none | All functions present → COMPLETE COGNITION BASELINE |
| EXEC DENSE | none | All functions present (transformed) → TOTAL STRUCTURAL INTERROGATION |
| INVESTIGATION | #1, #2, #3, #4, #7, #11 | Eliminates orchestration, synthesis, compound detection → EVIDENCE VERIFICATION |

BOARDROOM and INVESTIGATION both have absences, but DIFFERENT absences. BOARDROOM eliminates operational functions (temporal, trail, blockage). INVESTIGATION eliminates interpretive functions (orchestration, synthesis, compound). This proves they are distinct cognitive architectures, not altitude variations of the same architecture.
