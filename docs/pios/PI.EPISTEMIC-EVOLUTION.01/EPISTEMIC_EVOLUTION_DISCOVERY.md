| Stage | Directional progress? | Maturation? |

| Qualification | S0→S1→S2; governed, gated, hard to reverse | YES |

- **Knowledge Evolution = maturation** (directional, gated, mostly monotonic)

Relationship to time:
```
maturation ⊂ temporal change
(all maturation is temporal; not all temporal change is maturation)
```
Maturation is *directional* temporal evolution. The user's instinct — "common denominator is maturation, not time" — is right for the knowledge classes and is the discriminator between the two halves of the split.
