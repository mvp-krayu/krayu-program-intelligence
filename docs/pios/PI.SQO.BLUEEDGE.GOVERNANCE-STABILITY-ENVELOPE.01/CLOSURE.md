## Key Findings

1. **Dependency depth is the primary governance risk.** No formal
   depth limit exists. Recommended: impose depth limit of 2.

2. **Compound pressure is more dangerous than single-dimension
   pressure.** 3+ dimensions in PRESSURE zone simultaneously
   can trigger OVERLOADED even when each dimension appears safe.

3. **Replay and rollback determinism never degrade.** They are
   structural properties, not scaling properties. Only CLARITY
   degrades under pressure.

4. **Coexistence checks grow quadratically** with overlay count.
   This is the primary driver of governance complexity.

5. **Full S3 achievement requires multi-entry packages** (13
   domains, 10-package limit). The architectural limit prevents
   1:1 domain-to-package mapping for full coverage.

6. **Observability is the first property to degrade.** It degrades
   at lower pressure levels than replay or rollback integrity.

7. **Recovery to certified baseline is ALWAYS possible.** The
   immutability of the certified baseline is the ultimate
   governance safety net.
