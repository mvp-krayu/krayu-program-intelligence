# HYDRATED State and Q-State Evolution

> **The system's most important conceptual contribution — graduated trustworthiness with honest disclosure.**

---

## What HYDRATED Means

HYDRATED is the state where semantic reconstruction has achieved **high internal coherence and operational usefulness** but without complete structural grounding proof for every semantic claim.

**HYDRATED is NOT a degraded state.** It is a legitimate, productive state that recognizes:
- Semantic continuity from multiple corroborating sources has value
- Operational usefulness does not require structural proof
- Executive intelligence can be delivered honestly with qualification disclosure
- Progressive grounding can proceed without blocking semantic utility

## Why HYDRATED Emerged

The system was producing rich semantic intelligence but had no name for its trustworthiness level. Without a name:
- The output was treated as either "complete" (wrong) or "deficient" (harmful)
- Governance pressure pushed toward blocking rendering until full proof existed
- The most commercially valuable output was at risk of being hidden

Naming the state resolved the confusion: HYDRATED output renders with Q-02/Q-03 disclosure. This is explicitly legitimate.

## State Progression

```
NONE ────────── No semantic reconstruction
  ↓
HYDRATED ────── Semantic reconstruction complete. Q-02/Q-03.
  ↓               LENS v2 renderable with disclosure.
RECONCILED ──── Crosswalk correspondence established.
  ↓               Semantic domains mapped to registries.
PARTIAL ─────── At least one structural anchor per domain cluster.
  ↓               Vault or topology evidence for primary domains.
STRONG ──────── Majority of domains structurally grounded.
  ↓               Q-01 achievable for grounded subset.
EXACT ───────── All domains structurally grounded.
  ↓               Full replay verification passed.
CERTIFIED ───── Governance certification issued.
  ↓               Immutable certification artifact.
AUTHORITY ───── Publication eligibility granted.
  ↓               Downstream binding authorized.
LENS ────────── Bound to runtime surfaces.
                  Authoritative rendering. No disclosure required.
```

## Q-Class Governance

| Q-Class | Name | Grounding Level | Disclosure |
|---|---|---|---|
| Q-01 | Fully Grounded | All domains structurally proven | None required |
| Q-02 | Partially Grounded | Some domains grounded, some semantic-only | Mandatory partial disclosure |
| Q-03 | Semantic Only | No structural grounding | Full semantic-only disclosure |
| Q-04 | Unqualified | Insufficient data for qualification | Unqualified rendering |

**Current BlueEdge state:** Q-02 — partial grounding with mandatory disclosure.

### Q-02 Governance Amendment

The Q-02 governance amendment (Q02_GOVERNANCE_AMENDMENT.md) is **LOCKED**. It defines:
- Q-02 rendering is explicitly legitimate
- Disclosure is mandatory, not optional
- Q-02 does not imply deficiency — it implies honest communication
- Q-class is deterministic from grounding ratio (not human judgment)
