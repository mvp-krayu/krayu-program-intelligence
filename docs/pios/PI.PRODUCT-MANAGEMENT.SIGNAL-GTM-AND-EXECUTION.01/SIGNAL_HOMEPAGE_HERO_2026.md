# Signäl Homepage Hero — 2026

> **Purpose:** Headline, sub-headline, CTA, and proof point options for signalpi.com hero section. All options are customer-ready. No internal architecture terminology.

---

## Alignment

```
COMMERCIAL WEDGE     Software Intelligence
                     "What your code structure means for execution."
                     → the hook, the first finding, the demo opener

PRODUCT CATEGORY     Structural Intelligence
                     "Governed structural truth about program execution."
                     → what the product delivers, how we describe the category

PLATFORM CATEGORY    Program Intelligence
                     "The discipline of governed structural execution assessment."
                     → the long-term brand, the destination
```

The hero leads with the wedge or the category. Never with the platform. The platform appears in the navigation or the "about" section — not the first 5 seconds.

---

## Hero Headlines

### Option A — Structural Truth

> **See what your tools cannot show you.**

Rationale: Directly challenges every existing tool. Creates curiosity. Implies a category gap that the visitor will want to understand. Short, memorable, repeatable.

### Option B — Execution Reality

> **The structural reality of your program's execution.**

Rationale: Defines the category in one line. "Structural reality" is the differentiator. "Your program" makes it personal. A CTO reads this and immediately asks "show me."

### Option C — Structural Intelligence (Category-Leading)

> **Structural Intelligence for software execution.**

Rationale: Plants the category name directly. Two concepts: "Structural Intelligence" (the category) and "software execution" (the domain). Clean, ownable, searchable.

### Option D — Consequence-Led

> **What your code structure actually means for execution.**

Rationale: This IS the Software Intelligence wedge — what code structure means. Bridges from what the buyer knows (code) to what they don't (execution consequences). Natural curiosity driver.

### Option E — Evidence-Led

> **Structural findings no other tool can produce.**

Rationale: Competitive positioning in 8 words. "Structural findings" names what you get. "No other tool" names why. Aggressive but defensible.
