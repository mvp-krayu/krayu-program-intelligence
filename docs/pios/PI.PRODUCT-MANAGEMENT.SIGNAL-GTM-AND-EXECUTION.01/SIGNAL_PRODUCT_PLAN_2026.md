## Per-SKU Product Definition

### SKU: SA — Signäl Structural Assessment

| Field | Definition |
|-------|-----------|
| **Buyer** | CTO, VP Engineering, Transformation Director. Secondary: PMO Director. |
| **Problem** | The buyer knows their program has structural bottlenecks — changes that should take days take weeks, modernizations stall, scaling doesn't improve throughput. They can feel it. They cannot prove it. Every existing tool shows planned reality or code quality — nothing shows structural execution reality. |
| **Outcome** | The buyer receives governed structural proof: where fragility concentrates, how changes propagate, what couples resist evolution, whether an operational ceiling exists. Evidence they can present to their board, steering committee, or architecture review board. In days, not months. |
| **Deliverables** | |
| LENS Access | BOARDROOM + BALANCED cognitive modes. Interactive structural topology with pressure zones. Named structural findings with severity. Software Intelligence consequence analysis (9 surfaces). |
| Structural Verdict | 9-chapter governed document. Named findings with 4 fields (observed/matters/operational implication/leadership implication). Board-ready. |
| Evidence Record | Governed structural snapshot: posture, confidence envelope, topology capture, governance boundary. Auditable. |
| Advisory Session | 60-90 min walkthrough: topology, findings, consequences, verdict. |
| **Packaging** | Per-program, per-engagement advisory fee. Not SaaS. Not subscription. Delivered in 3-5 business days. |
| **Success Criteria** | (1) Buyer can present Structural Verdict to their board without additional interpretation. (2) At least one finding creates buyer recognition ("yes, we've been experiencing this"). (3) Buyer requests follow-up conversation about Continuous Intelligence within 30 days. |

---

### SKU: SA-DD — Signäl Structural Due Diligence

| Field | Definition |
|-------|-----------|
| **Buyer** | PE Partner, M&A Technical Lead, Corporate Development. |
| **Problem** | Manual due diligence takes 4-6 weeks and delivers consultant opinions. Deal timelines don't accommodate this. The investment committee needs structural risk assessment with evidence lineage, not expert judgment. |
| **Outcome** | Structural risk assessment of the acquisition target delivered within deal timelines. Investment committee-ready Structural Verdict with operational ceiling analysis, structural convergence detection, and evidence-traced findings. Reproducible — run it again, get the same result. |
| **Deliverables** | |
| Everything in SA | Full SA deliverable set. |
| Full LENS Access | All 4 cognitive modes: BOARDROOM, BALANCED, DENSE, OPERATOR (with Investigation Protocol). |
| Guided Investigation | 36 governed structural queries with evidence-traced answers. Self-service structural exploration within LENS. |
| Investigation Trail | Governed record of all structural investigations — questions explored, evidence cited, governance boundaries disclosed. Appendable to investment committee packages. |
| Investigation Protocol | Within OPERATOR mode: structural verification panels, evidence completeness assessment, per-qualification-target pass/fail. |
| **Packaging** | Per-target, per-engagement premium fee. Deal-timeline delivery (3-5 business days). |
| **Success Criteria** | (1) Structural Verdict accepted by investment committee as structural risk input. (2) Findings inform deal valuation or post-acquisition planning. (3) Delivery within committed deal timeline. (4) Buyer requests portfolio-wide assessment (SE conversion path). |

### SKU: SC — Signäl Continuous Intelligence

| Field | Definition |
|-------|-----------|
| **Buyer** | CTO, VP Engineering, Chief Architect. |
| **Problem** | A point-in-time assessment shows structural reality at a snapshot. But structure evolves — architectural investments change posture, new coupling patterns emerge, modernization progress needs structural verification. The buyer needs ongoing structural visibility, not a one-shot report. |
| **Outcome** | Continuous structural intelligence as a permanent organizational capability. Track how structural posture evolves across assessment runs. Self-service investigation whenever structural questions arise. Structural escalation when the evidence warrants deeper analysis. |
| **Deliverables** | |
| Everything in SA-DD | Full SA-DD deliverable set (all 4 modes, investigation, trail export). |
| Multiple Assessment Runs | Run structural analysis on demand or on schedule. Compare posture across runs. |
| Qualification Progression | Track qualification state evolution across assessments. Measure structural improvement. |
| Structural Escalation | Evidence-triggered governed interpretive depth — when the structure warrants deeper investigation, the system enables governed projection under bounded authority. |
| Quarterly Advisory | Periodic structural evolution review with Signäl team. Posture comparison, trend analysis, decision support. |
| **Packaging** | Annual subscription. Covers: platform access, assessment runs, quarterly advisory, all intelligence layers. |
| **Success Criteria** | (1) Customer runs at least 2 assessments within first 6 months. (2) Structural posture delta is measurable between runs. (3) Customer cites Signäl findings in architectural decision-making. (4) Renewal rate >80%. |

### SKU: SE — Signäl Enterprise Intelligence

| Field | Definition |
|-------|-----------|
| **Buyer** | Enterprise CTO, Chief Architect, PE Portfolio Management. |
| **Problem** | Structural intelligence needs to become organizational infrastructure — role-based access for different stakeholders, governed qualification workflows with operator authority, structural visibility across multiple programs, and extensibility for domain-specific intelligence modules. |
| **Outcome** | Enterprise-grade structural intelligence operating system. Multiple programs assessed through a single platform. Operator authority workflows with RBAC. Governed qualification state machine with audit lineage. Foundation for domain module marketplace. |
| **Deliverables** | |
| Everything in SC | Full SC deliverable set. |
| Operator Authority Workflow | 12 governed actions, role-based access control. Operator-governed qualification mutations with audit trail. |
| RBAC | 5 roles: VIEWER, ANALYST, OPERATOR, ADMINISTRATOR, AUTHORITY. Each sees appropriate depth and has appropriate action authority. |
| Multi-Program Topology (future) | Structural intelligence across programs. Cross-program dependency visibility. Portfolio-level structural posture. |
| Custom Domain Modules (future) | Activate domain-specific intelligence modules: M&A Intelligence, Portfolio Intelligence, PM Intelligence, Organizational Intelligence. |
| **Packaging** | Multi-year platform license. Enterprise pricing. Unlimited assessment runs, all intelligence layers, dedicated advisory, SLA. |
| **Success Criteria** | (1) Platform deployed with RBAC across at least 3 stakeholder roles. (2) Operator workflow exercised for qualification governance. (3) At least 2 programs assessed through single platform instance. (4) Multi-year renewal. |

## Product Decision Register

Decisions required before or during implementation:

| # | Decision | Options | Recommendation | Impact |
|---|----------|---------|----------------|--------|
| D1 | Capability-level topology for Tier 2 | (A) Domain/cluster is sufficient — update product language. (B) Port capability drill-down from legacy. | **A** — domain/cluster is an architectural improvement, not a regression. Update product language to "semantic cluster topology." | Saves 1-2 weeks if accepted. |
| D2 | Numeric ceiling score | (A) Add derived numeric ceiling score. (B) Update product language to governed ceiling posture with driver attribution. | **B** — qualitative ceiling with drivers is more valuable than a fake number. | Product language update only. |
| D3 | "Golden Query" feature | (A) Build domain-scoped golden query. (B) Retire in favor of "Guided Structural Interrogation" (which already exists and is stronger). | **B** — the guided query system with 36 queries and structural escalation is the better product. | Saves 1-2 weeks. |
| D4 | Evidence vault access model | (A) Raw artifact browsing. (B) Governed evidence inspection (current model). | **B** — raw vault access bypasses governance and is architecturally incorrect. Current governed view is the right product. | No code change needed. |
| D5 | Claim+Derived+TraceDepth output format | (A) Build this format. (B) Retire in favor of governed evidence trail export (InterrogationTrailBuilder). | **B** — the trail builder produces a stronger, governed artifact. | Saves implementation. |
| D6 | Multi-run comparison scope for SC | (A) Full posture delta dashboard. (B) Run-over-run Structural Verdict comparison (diff). (C) Posture trend line only. | **C for launch, A as roadmap** — posture trend gives the buyer "is it getting better?" without building a comparison dashboard. | Reduces Tier 2 gap from 2 weeks to 3-4 days. |

**Recommendation:** Accept D1-D5 as Option B (update product language to match stronger implementation). Accept D6 as Option C for launch. These decisions collectively save 5-6 weeks of implementation and produce a more accurate product.
