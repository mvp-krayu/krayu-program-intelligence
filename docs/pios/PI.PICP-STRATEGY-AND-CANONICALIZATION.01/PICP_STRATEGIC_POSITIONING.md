## 2. Candidate Assessment

### 2.1 Report Generator

**Definition:** A system that generates executive reports from structural evidence.

**Why REJECTED:**
- This was the pre-discovery understanding. PI.EXECUTIVE-COGNITION-RUNTIME.01 proved it is factually incorrect.
- Reports are ONE of EIGHT projection families. They are the medium, not the product.
- A "Report Generator" identity constrains PI to document production — a commodity category.
- The 55/20/19/6 analysis proved that only 6% of a report is genuine rendering. The remaining 94% is structural intelligence + narrative machinery + cognition. A "Report Generator" claims credit for the 6%.

### 2.2 Executive Reporting Platform

**Definition:** A platform that produces multiple types of executive reports and deliverables.

**Why REJECTED:**
- Better than "Report Generator" — acknowledges multiple output types.
- But still sells the medium. "Executive Reporting" positions PI alongside tools that generate slide decks and board packs.
- The word "Reporting" implies the value is in the output format, not the intelligence.
- This identity competes with consulting firms and presentation tools — the wrong competitive frame.

### 2.3 Executive Cognition Runtime

**Definition:** A runtime that produces structured executive cognition independently of any delivery format.

**Why PARTIALLY CORRECT:**
- This was the discovery of PI.EXECUTIVE-COGNITION-RUNTIME.01. It is architecturally correct.
- The recognition that "executive intelligence is a runtime layer, not a document format" is the fundamental insight.
- But "Executive" limits the scope. The cognition package serves financial, engineering, and operational audiences — not just executives.
- After terminology reconciliation (ECP → PICP, ECR → PICR), the "Executive" qualifier is removed.

### 2.4 Program Intelligence Cognition Platform

**Definition:** A platform that produces structured Program Intelligence cognition — audience-independent, deterministically derived, format-independent — and renders it through governed projection families for specific audiences.

**Why CORRECT:**
- This identity captures the full discovery: cognition is the product, surfaces are the medium.
- "Program Intelligence" anchors to the established category identity (frozen 2026-05-17).
- "Cognition" describes what is produced — structured intelligence objects, not reports.
- "Platform" acknowledges the marketplace dimension — multiple modules, multiple projections, extensible.
- This identity is:
  - Unique — no competitor sells structured cognition packages
  - Accurate — matches what the pipeline actually produces
  - Extensible — accommodates future modules and projection families
  - Category-consistent — sits within the Program Intelligence discipline

### 2.5 Marketplace for Cognition Consumers

**Definition:** A marketplace where consumers purchase access to cognition modules and projection families.

**Why PARTIALLY CORRECT:**
- The marketplace IS a component of the platform, not the platform itself.
- "Marketplace" is the distribution and commercial mechanism, not the identity.
- The PICP exists before the marketplace — the marketplace enables access to domain-specific enrichment and specialized projections.
- This is the COMMERCIAL MODEL, not the IDENTITY.

## 4. The Discovery Arc

Looking at the three-stream discovery sequence:

### Stream 1: PI.EXECUTIVE-INTELLIGENCE-REPORT-COMPILER.01
**Discovery:** The report is 55% deterministic pipeline output / 20% templateable / 25% "consulting craft."
**Insight:** Most of the report is already automated. The gap is the remaining 25%.

### Stream 2: PI.EXECUTIVE-COGNITION-RUNTIME.01
**Discovery:** 77% of the "consulting craft" is latent PI cognition that was never formalized. Only 6% is genuine rendering.
**Insight:** The report is merely one projection. The cognition is the product.

### Stream 3: PI.PICP-STRATEGY-AND-CANONICALIZATION.01 (this stream)
**Discovery:** "Executive" was a residual label from the report-centric model. The cognition package is Program Intelligence cognition, not executive cognition.
**Insight:** The platform is a Program Intelligence Cognition Platform. The marketplace sells modules and projections, not reports.

### The Arc

```
"We make reports"
     ↓
"We compile intelligence into reports"
     ↓
"We produce cognition, then project it into many surfaces"
     ↓
"The cognition is the product. The surfaces are the medium."
```

Each step was an evidence-driven architectural discovery, not a branding exercise. The positioning follows from what the system actually produces, verified by forensic analysis of real pipeline outputs.
