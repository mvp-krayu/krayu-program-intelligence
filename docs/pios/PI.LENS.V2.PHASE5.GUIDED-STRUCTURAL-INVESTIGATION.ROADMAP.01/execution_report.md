## 5. Design Decisions

### Structural Exploration as Primary Interaction Model
Topology + evidence traversal is the center of gravity, not query. Query is secondary (5B.2). This preserves the product's differentiation as a structural cognition instrument rather than an LLM-over-dashboard.

### Investigative Authority as Governance Layer
The binary deterministic/interpretive split left no room for topology-derived navigation. Investigative authority fills this gap: the system selects which structural connections to surface based on navigation position, but selection criteria are topology-derived, not inference-derived.

### Temporal Intelligence as Transition Point
5A.5 is not merely another sub-phase. It bridges LENS v2 from assessment product to operational intelligence system. Without temporal cognition, the product remains a static snapshot explorer.

### 5A.3 Governance Boundary
Structural blockage visibility explicitly prohibits remediation prioritization, ranked actions, optimization advice, and prescription. The system shows the wall — it does not point at the door.

### Organizational Psychology Prohibition
5B explicitly prohibits team behavior inference, cultural diagnosis, leadership quality interpretation, and personnel attribution. The system has structural evidence about code and domains. It has zero evidence about human behavior.

### 5A.6 Positioned Last
The integrated investigation flow ships after temporal intelligence because it must integrate all four prior capabilities into a coherent session including temporal navigation.
